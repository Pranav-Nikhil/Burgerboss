History of Burger

The hamburger's origins trace back to the late 19th century. While ground meat patties have been eaten for centuries, the concept of serving them in a bun gained popularity in the United States. Fast-food chains played a crucial role in the burger's global popularity.From its American origins to its worldwide adoption, the burger's journey is a testament to its delicious simplicity and adaptable nature."At Burger Boss, we're dedicated to crafting burgers that transcend ordinary expectations.
